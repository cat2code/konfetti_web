About Us

We are six passionate musicians and academics from Lund who love creating magic on the dance floor. Our group started as a student house band in the University-city of Lund and has since evolved into a fully-fledged party band featuring one doctor and five engineers.

Founded in 2025, we’ve played everything from formal balls and clubs to weddings and private events.

Formerly Malmö Nation’s house band (2021–2024).
